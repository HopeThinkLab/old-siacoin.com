Jobs
Working at Nebulous
Nebulous is the parent company of Sia, and funds the majority of Sia's development. We are looking primarily for full time employees, however are also open to discussing alternate arrangements.

You can apply by sending an email to david@nebulouslabs.com

UI/UX Engineer
We are looking for someone to take the reigns for the design and implementation of our website and user interface. You will need to write most of the HTML, CSS, and Javascript yourself. We are looking for someone who can pull together all of the different aspects of Sia into a simple and consistent experience for the user. We have a preference for minimalism and meticulous design.

Wallet Engineer
We are looking for someone to take over the wallet implementation of Sia. This is primarily backend work, and indcludes adding features like payment channels, accounts, transaction histories, and integrating a way to convert between Bitcoin and Siacoin. There are a lot of cool places that the wallet could go and we need someone to spearhead that effort.

Decentralization Engineer
We are looking for someone to help us build a cutting edge and extremely efficient decentralized network. This includes designing and implementing algorithms for choosing hosts from the network, designing and implementing algorithms that help hosts to choose prices for their storage and bandwidth, and optimizing software to maximize scalability. Because Sia operates in a decentralized context, you will need to be able to design algorithms that are stable and successful under harsh adversarial environments. This position requires substantial knowledge of cryptocurrency systems, especially Bitcoin and the various proposals that exist for modifications to Bitcoin. General knowledge of distributed systems is also preferred.
